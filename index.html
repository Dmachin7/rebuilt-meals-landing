FAQ

Common Questions

Am I locked into a subscription?

▾

No. You can skip a week or cancel anytime from your account dashboard. No cancellation fees, no commitments. We earn your business every week.

How does the free side item work?

▾

As an active subscriber, every box automatically includes one free side item (like roasted sweet potatoes, rice, or seasonal veggies). It's our way of saying thanks for sticking with us.

Can I pick my own meals or do you choose for me?

▾

Both! You can browse the weekly menu and pick your favorites, or let our team curate a box based on your preferences. Either way, the menu changes every week.

How is this different from Factor or HelloFresh?

▾

We're a local Tampa Bay kitchen, not a national shipping operation. Your meals are prepared fresh (never frozen), cooked in olive oil, and delivered same-day or available for pickup. National services ship frozen meals across the country — we deliver freshness across the Bay.

What dietary options do you offer?

▾

Our weekly menu includes keto, high-protein, paleo, and balanced options. You can filter by dietary preference when choosing your meals. We also accommodate common allergies — just note them in your profile.

Where are the pickup locations?

▾

We have pickup spots across Tampa Bay including South Tampa, Clearwater, and St. Petersburg. Check the map on our locations page for the one nearest you. Pickup is available on delivery days.
