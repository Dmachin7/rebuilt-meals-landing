FAQ

Common Questions

What is ReBuilt Meals?

▾

ReBuilt Meals is a Tampa-based meal prep delivery service that provides fresh, chef-prepared, macro balanced meals delivered straight to your door every week. Our menu rotates weekly so you always have something new and exciting to look forward to. No seed oils, never frozen, always delicious.

How Often Can I Skip a Week?

▾

You can skip any week from your customer dashboard. Simply log in and manage your subscription preferences. We ask that you submit changes before the weekly cutoff deadline so your order can be adjusted in time. There are no penalties for skipping — life happens!

How Does the Side Menu Work?

▾

Every week subscribers get access to add-on side items — think roasted veggies, rice, or on-the-go snacks — at checkout. One free treat is included automatically with every order starting from week two of your subscription. Add extras to any order to round out your nutrition plan.

Can I Customize My Meals?

▾

Yes! You can select from the weekly menu to build your own box. We offer protein, carb, and fat macro profiles across all meals so you can match your fitness and dietary goals. We also offer double protein upgrades on select meals for athletes and high-output individuals.

How is This Different from Factor Meals?

▾

Unlike national shipping companies, ReBuilt is a local Tampa kitchen. Your meals are prepared fresh and never frozen, delivered directly from our kitchen to your door — meaning better taste, better ingredients, and a faster turnaround than big-box national brands. You're also supporting a local Tampa business.

Where Are the Pickup Locations?

▾

We have multiple pickup locations across Tampa Bay including South Tampa, Clearwater, Wesley Chapel, Carrollwood, and more. We also offer direct home delivery across the greater Tampa area. Check our website for the full list of current pickup spots and delivery zones.
